B: So yes. Harder to preserve digital stuff because files tend to corrupt on their own. There’s something called bit rot, where bits get flipped around if you don’t keep an eye on them. And so you have to like- keep up with that software and keep transferring things to the new generation of LTO tape.

Versus like- we have books from like the sixteenth century, and as long as you store them in a cold enough climate with low humidity like, they’re gonna be fine.
