IS 270 Systems and Infrastructures

Hannah AlNemri, Lynelle Collo, Bob Emrich, Toni Vang

Contribution Statement

Our group worked together throughout each assignment and aspect of the Specialization Dossier. Hannah contacted our LIS professional and set up the interview. Each of us worked on questions to ask her, and the interview was led by Hannah and conducted by Lynelle and Toni, as Bob was in class. Toni created the transcription, Lynelle built the website, Bob did annotated bibliography, and Hannah created the slideshow presentation. Each of us contributed to writing out the dossier, searching for sources, looking for additional job descriptions, and proofreading the project. Additionally, we each made contributions and suggestions to each others’ areas, discussed them over text, calls, and in class, and incorporated all of our thoughts and ideas in the final project. While we each had individual pieces, our group each had a hand in the entire project, and we are very pleased with how we worked together to bring this project together.
